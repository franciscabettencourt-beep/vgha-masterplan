# Vilalara Grand Hotel Algarve — Strategic Dashboard (2026–2031)

Interactive strategic dashboard built to support executive decision-making, performance tracking, and long-term growth planning for Vilalara Grand Hotel Algarve.

## 2026 Target Snapshot

* Revenue: €11,941,356
* Room Nights: 24,366
* ADR: €490

## Overview

This dashboard consolidates real operational, financial, and commercial data into a single navigable interface, designed to align leadership, departments, and strategic initiatives.

## Structure

### 1. Vision

* 6 strategic pillars
* Current performance baseline (2025 actuals)
* Interactive timeline (2026 → 2031)

### 2. Diagnostic

* Full P&L (March 2026)
* STR benchmarking vs comp set
* Occupancy positioning (5th of 5)
* OTB pacing by month (risk signals)
* Top feeder markets (real data)
* Headcount: 143 FTE

### 3. Financials

* Revenue evolution (2024 → 2031)
* KPI tracking:

  * ADR
  * RevPAR
  * Direct channel share
  * ROAS
* Revenue mix transformation
* Paid media investment strategy

### 4. Roadmap

* Year-by-year execution (2026 → 2031)
* Defined mission and targets per year
* Action plan with quantified impact

### 5. Departments

9 operational areas:

* Marketing
* Revenue & Commercial
* F&B
* Wellness & Spa
* Front Office
* Housekeeping
* Guest Experience
* People & Culture
* Back Office & Finance

Each includes:

* KPI framework
* Performance diagnosis
* Prioritized action plan (Critical / High / Medium)

### 6. Markets

* Market-level profitability analysis (CPA, ADR, margin)
* Competitive benchmarking
* Strategic partnerships:

  * Forbes Travel Guide
  * Virtuoso
  * Leading Hotels of the World
  * Condé Nast Traveler

### 7. KPIs & Governance

* Master KPI table by year and ownership
* Department-level tracking cadence
* Weekly and monthly taskforce structure

## Deployment

1. Upload `index.html` to repository
2. Enable GitHub Pages
3. Access via public or private URL

## Purpose

This dashboard is designed to:

* Drive revenue growth and profitability
* Improve occupancy positioning within comp set
* Strengthen direct channel performance
* Align departments under a unified strategy
* Enable data-driven decision-making at executive level

## Confidentiality

This project contains sensitive operational and financial data intended for internal strategic use only.
